# Price Information

This document describes how prices are calculated and displayed within the Welsh Street Exchange.

### Price Calculation

Welsh Street Exchange uses an automated market maker (AMM) constant-product formula to determine exchange rates between WELSH and STREET tokens.

STREET price derives from the liquidity pool ratio:

```
street_price_usd = welsh_price_usd / (street_reserves / welsh_reserves)
```

**Price Components:**
- **WELSH Price**: Retrieved from external market data (ALEX exchange)
- **STREET Price**: Calculated from pool reserve ratios
- **Pool Ratio**: Determined by automated constant-product formula

### Premium/Discount Mechanism

The protocol tracks reserve changes to reflect supply and demand:

```
welsh_dex_premium = new_welsh_reserves / previous_welsh_reserves
```

This mechanism is inherent to constant-product AMM design and operates autonomously without human intervention.

## Time-Weighted Average Price (TWAP)

The system aggregates price data into 5-minute intervals to provide smoothed price information:

- **Time Bucket**: 5-minute intervals
- **Aggregation**: Average price across all swaps in the interval
- **Statistics**: Min, max, first, and last price per interval

TWAP data reflects historical trading activity and does not predict future price movements.

## Volume Tracking

Trading volume is tracked on a rolling 24-hour basis:

- **WELSH Volume**: Total WELSH traded in 24-hour period
- **STREET Volume**: Total STREET traded in 24-hour period
- **USD Volume**: Calculated using current WELSH market price
- **Trade Count**: Number of successful swaps

Volume data updates automatically with each swap transaction.

## Technical Notes

**No Price Oracle**: The protocol does not use price oracles for smart contract operations. All pricing is determined by AMM mathematics.

**Autonomous Operation**: Price calculations occur automatically via database triggers.

**External Markets**: WELSH price data is sourced from external exchanges via API.
