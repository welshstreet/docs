WELSH implements a **fixed supply model of 10 billion tokens**, creating digital scarcity reminiscent of Bitcoin's capped supply approach. This design choice differentiates WELSH from inflationary meme coins and positions it as a potential store of value within the meme coin sector.

- **Scarcity Premium**: Limited supply can drive value appreciation over time
- **Anti-Dilution**: Holders maintain their percentage ownership indefinitely
- **Institutional Appeal**: Fixed supply attracts serious investors alongside retail participants
