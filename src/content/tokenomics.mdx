## Community Distribution

The initial 5 billion STREET mint (50% of total supply) follows a strategic allocation designed to bootstrap the ecosystem while ensuring fair distribution:

```clarity
;; STREET MINT ALLOCATION (5 Billion Total)
(define-constant STREET_MINT_CAP u5000000000000000) ;; 5B tokens (50% of supply)

(define-public (street-mint (amount uint))
    (begin
        (asserts! (<= (+ (var-get street-minted) amount) STREET_MINT_CAP) ERR_EXCEEDS_STREET_MINT_CAP)
        (try! (ft-mint? street amount tx-sender)) ;; Mints to deployer for distribution
    )
)
```

## The Kill Switch Decision

STREET features a unique **one-way kill switch** that gives the community ultimate control over tokenomics. This irreversible decision allows transitioning from DOGE-style infinite supply to Bitcoin-style fixed supply. The idea is to start with infinite supply for growth and adoption. At any time the community can can decide when to cap the supply. It's one-time switched, though. Once capped, it's permanently capped.

```clarity
;; KILL SWITCH MECHANISM (ONE-WAY ONLY)
(define-data-var kill-switch bool false) ;; Default: infinite supply
(define-constant TOKEN_SUPPLY u10000000000000000) ;; 10B tokens (parity with WELSH)

(define-public (flip-kill-switch)
  (begin
    (asserts! (is-eq (var-get kill-switch) false) ERR_KILL_SWITCH_FLIPPED)
    (var-set kill-switch true) ;; CANNOT BE REVERSED
    (ok {kill-switch: true})
  )
)

;; SUPPLY LOGIC: INFINITE OR CAPPED AT 10B (WELSH PARITY)
(asserts!
  (or
    (not (var-get kill-switch))  ;; FALSE = Infinite supply (DOGE model)
    (<= (+ (ft-get-supply street) amount) TOKEN_SUPPLY)  ;; TRUE = 10B cap (WELSH parity)
  )
  ERR_EXCEEDS_TOTAL_SUPPLY)
```

<Callout type="important">Even if the kill switch is activated immediately, STREET still emits until reaching parity with Welshcorgicoin's 10B supply.</Callout>

## The Great Debate: Infinite Supply vs Fixed Supply

But what if we don;t want to cap the supply.  Welsh is already a fixed-supply community coin.  Street isn't designed to be a community coin.  Street is a meme rewards token designed to boost welsh.  If the supply remains infinite, that means Street Rewards to Welsh Credit holders will last forever.  Does it sound crazy?  Well, there's another famous dog meme with an infinite supply: DOGE


**Infinite Supply (Default - DOGE Model)**

**Pros:**
- ‚úÖ **Perpetual LP rewards** - Liquidity providers earn forever
- ‚úÖ **Sustainable yield** - Never-ending incentive to provide liquidity
- ‚úÖ **Proven model** - DOGE reached $80B+ market cap with infinite supply
- ‚úÖ **Anti-hoarding** - Encourages usage over speculation
- ‚úÖ **Economic growth** - Accommodates expanding user base

**Cons:**
- ‚ùå **Inflation pressure** - Constant new token creation
- ‚ùå **Dilution effects** - Existing holders get smaller percentage over time
- ‚ùå **Store of value concerns** - Harder to maintain purchasing power

**üîí Fixed Supply (Kill Switch Activated - Bitcoin Model)**

**Pros:**
- ‚úÖ **Scarcity premium** - Limited supply can drive value appreciation
- ‚úÖ **Store of value** - No inflation reduces purchasing power erosion
- ‚úÖ **Speculation appeal** - Fixed cap attracts different investor class
- ‚úÖ **Parity with WELSH** - 10B STREET matches 10B WelshCorgiCoin supply

**Cons:**
- ‚ùå **Finite LP rewards** - Eventually no new tokens to distribute
- ‚ùå **Yield sustainability** - Long-term LP incentive challenges
- ‚ùå **Growth limitations** - Fixed supply may constrain ecosystem expansion
- ‚ùå **Early adopter bias** - Benefits early participants disproportionately


Emitting 10,000 tokens every Bitcoin block until the gives community ample time to mature governance and make an informed decision.

To the best of our knowledge, no one has ever launched an infinite supply token with the sole purpose of boosting a fixed-supply token.
